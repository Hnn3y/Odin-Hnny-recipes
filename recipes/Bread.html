Swedish Coffee Bread Preparation

Description

  • This is a delicious Swedish coffee bread recipe converted for use with a bread maker. Enjoy it especially on Christmas morning. I hope your family enjoys this as much as mine has.

ingredients

    • 1 cup milk
    • ½ teaspoon salt
    • 1 egg yolk
    • 2 tablespoons softened butter
    • 3 cups all-purpose flour
    • ⅓ cup sugar
    • 1 (.25 ounce) envelope active dry yeast
    • 3 teaspoons ground cardamom
    • 2 egg whites,
    • slightly beaten
    • pearl sugar,
    • or other decorative sugar
